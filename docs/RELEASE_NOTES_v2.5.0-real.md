# Release Notes — v2.5.0-real

`v2.5.0-real` adds the Research Intake and Source Workflow Layer.

## Highlights

- New `/v2-live/research` page.
- Source registry for research inputs.
- Research queue for operator review flow.
- Source notes for claims, support/contradiction, uncertainty, and interpretation.
- Evidence candidates with credibility, relevance, freshness, direction, and uncertainty scoring.
- Human-controlled conversion from candidate to strategy evidence.
- Freshness/staleness warnings.
- Thesis comparison summaries.
- Research exports in JSON, Markdown, and CSV.
- Research actions audit logged.
- Tests and docs updated.

## Safety

No real order placement occurred during validation. No real order cancellation occurred during validation. Research, evidence, and thesis comparison objects do not submit, sign, approve, arm, or cancel live orders.
